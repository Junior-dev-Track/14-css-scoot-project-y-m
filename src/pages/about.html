FAQs

How it works

How do I download the app?

To download the Scoot app, you can search “Scoot” in both the App and Google Play stores. An even simpler way to do it would be to click the relevant link at the bottom of this page and you’ll be re-directed to the correct page.

Can I find a nearby Scoots?

Yes, you can find nearby Scoots by using the location feature within the Scoot app. Simply enable location services on your device, and the app will show you the nearest available Scoots.

Do I need a license to ride?

Yes, you need a valid driver's license to ride a Scoot. Make sure to have your license with you whenever you plan to ride.

Safe driving

Should I wear a helmet?

Yes, please do! All cities have different laws. But we strongly strongly strongly recommend always wearing a helmet regardless of the local laws. We like you and we want you to be as safe as possible while Scooting.

How about the rules & regulations?

When it comes to rules and regulations, it's important to familiarize yourself with local laws regarding electric scooters. Some cities may have specific guidelines for riding scooters on public roads or sidewalks. Check with your local authorities or visit our website for more information on scooter regulations in your area.

What if I damage my Scoot?

If you damage your Scoot, please contact our customer support team as soon as possible. Depending on the extent of the damage, you may be responsible for repair costs. Our team will assist you in resolving the issue and getting back on the road safely.
